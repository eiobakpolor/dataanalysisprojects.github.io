Techtronix Innovation Analysis

This project was an exciting challenge, offering a unique opportunity to analyze a vast dataset from Techtronix Innovations, a global leader in the microchip and robotics industry. With customers spanning diverse sectors and regions—from bustling hubs in the United States to emerging markets in Asia—we unearthed valuable insights through detailed analysis and compelling visualizations. Our exploration revealed intriguing patterns, such as the dominance of the automotive sector in profitability and unexpected growth potential in underrepresented regions like Southeast Asia. By dissecting sales trends, customer preferences, and production inefficiencies, we identified actionable strategies to optimize inventory, enhance profitability, and guide strategic market expansion. This project not only highlighted the transformative power of data-driven decision-making but also underscored the importance of leveraging technology like Power BI for crafting dynamic and impactful business solutions.

T.T INC Analysis using SQL

This project explored supply chain optimization for T.T. Inc., a leader in consumer electronics. By analyzing a rich dataset of sales, products, and economic factors, we identified strategies to streamline inventory management and improve customer satisfaction. SQL analysis revealed trends such as the impact of promotions on sales and seasonal peaks in demand for specific product categories. Insights from GDP and inflation correlations with sales volumes informed strategic decisions. The analysis helped calculate optimal reorder levels, identify top-performing SKUs, and address overstock and understock issues. These findings provide actionable recommendations to enhance inventory efficiency and prepare T.T. Inc. for a successful supply chain strategy meeting.

DigitalPath Innovations Analysis using Tableau

This project focused on analyzing an A/B test conducted by DigitalPath Innovations to evaluate the performance of two webpage versions (A and B). Using Tableau, we explored key metrics like engagement, conversion rates, content interaction, and referral source impact. The analysis revealed that Version B consistently outperformed Version A, showing higher time spent on site, more actions taken, and better purchase completion rates, surpassing industry benchmarks. Content interaction insights indicated that users of Version B were more likely to watch videos and provide feedback, demonstrating stronger engagement. Additionally, referral source analysis highlighted differences in user behavior, offering strategies to target traffic more effectively. These insights provide clear recommendations for optimizing website design and improving both user satisfaction and business outcomes.
